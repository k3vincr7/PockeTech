📈 4–20 mA Basics

This is the foundation of instrumentation. If you understand this, you're ahead of most techs.

What is 4–20 mA?

It’s a signal used to represent a measurement.

  • 4 mA = 0%
  • 20 mA = 100%
Visual (Signal vs %)

Think of current like a progress bar.

4 mA → Empty (0%)

12 mA → Half (50%)

20 mA → Full (100%)

Quick Formula

% = (mA - 4) / 16 × 100
