🚦 On / Off Valve (DO / XV)
Discrete control using solenoids
What is this?

An On/Off valve is either OPEN or CLOSED. It does NOT modulate like a control valve.

How it Actually Works
🧠 PLC / DCS (DO Output)
Sends ON/OFF signal (energize or de-energize)
⚡ Solenoid Valve
Uses electrical signal to open or block air
💨 Air Supply
Air is routed depending on solenoid state
⚙️ Valve / Actuator
Fully opens or fully closes
Energized vs De-Energized
⚡ Energized
Solenoid shifts → air flows → valve changes position
🔌 De-Energized
Solenoid resets → valve returns to fail state
Fail States (CRITICAL)
❌ Fail Closed (FC)
No power or air → valve CLOSES
❌ Fail Open (FO)
No power or air → valve OPENS
❌ Fail Last (FL)
Valve stays where it was (less common)
Real Field Example
  • DO ON → Valve Opens
  • DO OFF → Valve Closes
  • Power loss → Valve goes to fail state
Quick Troubleshooting
⚠️ Valve not moving
→ Check solenoid energization (voltage)
⚠️ Solenoid clicking but no movement
→ Check air supply or tubing
⚠️ Valve moves opposite of command
→ Wiring reversed or wrong fail setup
⚠️ Valve stuck
→ Mechanical issue or actuator problem
Field Tip

Always check: Power → Solenoid → Air → Valve (in that order).
