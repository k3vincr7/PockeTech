⚙️ Control Valve (AO)
Test version — signal → air → movement
What is happening?

A control valve does NOT move from electricity directly. It moves using air, controlled by a signal.

How it Actually Works
🧠 PLC / DCS (Output)
Sends 4–20 mA signal (command)
🎛️ Positioner (I/P)
Converts signal → air pressure
💨 Air Supply
Provides the force to move actuator
⚙️ Valve / Actuator
Moves based on air pressure
Real Field Example
  • 4 mA → Closed
  • 12 mA → Half Open
  • 20 mA → Fully Open
Common Mistakes
  • Thinking signal moves valve ❌
  • Ignoring air system ❌
  • Air is the muscle ✅
Field Tip

No air = no movement. Always check supply, regulator, and tubing first.
