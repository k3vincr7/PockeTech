Our Story

Where Craft & Class Meet

Los Carnales is more than a bar — it's a destination. Born from a love of the game and a passion for exceptional spirits, we built a lounge where the precision of billiards meets the artistry of the cocktail.

Our tables are meticulously maintained, our cocktails are handcrafted with intention, and our atmosphere is designed to make every visit feel like an occasion worth remembering.
